About

Ahmet Demircan is a highly motivated Electrical and Computer Engineering Master's student at KTO Karatay University, with a strong foundation in electrical-electronic engineering from Nuh Naci Yazgan and an exchange program at Fontys University of Applied Sciences. He possesses a diverse skill set, including proficiency in Python, MATLAB, SQL, and C#. Ahmet has practical experience as a Technical Services Specialist at Mert Mattress Machinery, an Intern Avionics Systems Engineer at 12.HAVA ULAŞTIRMA ANA ÜS KOMUTANLIĞI, and an Intern Network Management Engineer at Halkbank. He is actively expanding his expertise through certifications in Cyber Security (IBM skill build cyber start), Transition to Web3 (Rise in WEB3 BOOTCAMP), and WebCrafters Full-Stack .NET (techcareer.net). Ahmet is a Turkish native speaker with strong English language skills, demonstrated by his C1 listening, C1 spoken production, C1 spoken interaction, B2 reading, and B2 writing abilities
